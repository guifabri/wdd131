Caracas City Guide - Site Plan

Site Name

Caracas City Guide

This name clearly identifies the city focus and the purpose of helping visitors find places and activities.

Optional domain: caracascityguide.org

Site Purpose

The site provides practical information for people exploring Caracas. It includes top places to visit, local tips, and a form to subscribe for updates.

Scenarios

  • Which places in Caracas are best for a first-time visitor?
  • How can I join the newsletter and receive local recommendations?

Color Schema

#0B3C5D: header, footer, and key headings.

#F4F6F8: page background and content section contrast.

#F18F01: action buttons and highlights.

Typography

Playfair Display: headings and titles.

Roboto: body text, form labels, and navigation.
